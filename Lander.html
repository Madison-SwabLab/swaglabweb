FAQ

Answers to common questions from merch teams

How is SwagLab.ai different from generic design tools? + –

Our model is fine‑tuned specifically for branded merchandise using thousands of professional apparel assets through a co‑founder’s library. It outputs brand‑safe, production‑ready results and integrates into B2B sales workflows.

Can I export files for print & embroidery? + –

Yes. Export high‑resolution mockups and vector artwork suitable for screen print, DTG, DTF, and embroidery. Color separations and sizing guides are built in.

What about IP and brand safety? + –

We respect client brand assets and provide guardrails to prevent unauthorized use of marks. Licensing terms are clear, with opt‑in dataset contributions and private instances available for enterprise.

Do you offer white‑label or API access? + –

Yes. Enterprise plans include white‑label portals, SSO, usage analytics, and API access for large distributors and partners.
